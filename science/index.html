Science Model Making Competition

A golden opportunity for young minds to showcase creativity and innovation!

About the competition

What to make?

Make a working science model of your choice. Solve problems with your ideas, and present your innovation at the event. Parents may guide, but the effort should be your own!

  • Independence and creativity encouraged
  • Short demo/explanation required
  • Bring finished model on GPL day

Eligibility

Participation is limited to children from our community, under the jurisdiction of Perne Kshetra.
